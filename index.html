Our History

A.B Cinema is located in Ughelli, Delta State, Nigeria. It was started by making skateboarding films as a teenager in Ozoro. After premiering his first full length film, New Flavors in 2014, A.B Cinema dove deeper into the narrative world and starting shooting feature films. After shooting 10 features in just 3 years, A.B Cinema transitioned into commercials, and music videos, working with artists like Saints Kelivn, Bishop, Pastor Akpovie Murphy and more. A.B Cinema has published several photo books for marriages, and all events and runs an independent photography studio. With a love for film, We enjoy capturing daily life on both Super 8 and 16mm.

Credit goes to A.B CINEMA. A.B Cenima is an award-winning cinematography, who has lensed projects all over the country. Over the last decade he has shot dozens of feature films, commercials, documentaries and short films.

What We Do

We offer professional media services like photography, videography, documentary, lighting, audio/video/live recordings and cinematic films for all event. We also render training services for Individuals organisations and churches on various professional media skills.

Our work covers many different types of photographic assignment, ranging from individual and group portraits, company events (product or service launches, company parties, conferences), annual report and location photographs, and corporate video production.

Photography is the art, application and practice of creating durable images by recording light or other electromagnetic radiation, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material such as photographic film. It is employed in many fields of science, manufacturing, and business, as well as its more direct uses for art, film and video production, recreational purposes, hobby, and mass communication

A large variety of photographic techniques and media are used in the process of capturing images for photography. These include the camera; stereoscopy; dualphotography; full-spectrum, ultraviolet and infrared media; light field photography; and other imaging techniques
